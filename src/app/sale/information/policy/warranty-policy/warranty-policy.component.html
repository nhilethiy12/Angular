Warranty policy

1

Warranty conditions

JFT only guarantees to repair and replace damaged parts of new vehicles in the following conditions: the vehicle operates in normal conditions, the spare parts are not good, the assembly error is not good.

believable

2

System Warranty

A powertrain warranty simply covers the components that help power and keep the vehicle moving, including the engine, transmission, and drivetrain. If any component fails or is damaged during the warranty period, the manufacturer will be responsible for the full cost of the repair or replacement. For repairs from other parts, you may have to pay for it yourself, even though the warranty period has not expired.

believable

3

Spare parts warranty

All genuine passenger car parts are covered by a two-year unlimited mileage warranty. The warranty period is calculated from the date of installation at the JFT store.

believable

4

Warranty extension

The "Warranty Extension Program" gives owners the opportunity to extend the warranty period from 1 year to 2 years so you can have complete peace of mind about your travel companion. So each customer who buys a new car will receive a 3-year factory warranty and have the opportunity to buy an additional warranty extension package of up to 2 years.

believable
